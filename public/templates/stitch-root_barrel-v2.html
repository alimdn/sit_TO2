Our Roots

Founded in 1948 by the Miller family, our farm began with a simple philosophy: if you treat the earth with respect, it will feed you for generations. We have never used synthetic fertilizers, preferring the slow, natural cycle of composting and crop rotation.

Root & Barrel isn't just about food; it's about people. We partner with over 40 local artisan producers to ensure that our marketplace supports the local economy and preserves heritage farming techniques.

We are currently implementing regenerative agriculture practices that actively sequester carbon back into our fields, ensuring our grandchildren will enjoy the same bounty we do today.

Why Organic?

Soil Health

Rich, living soil produces nutrient-dense food. Our biological approach ensures the earth remains vibrant and alive.

Pure Flavor

Without chemicals, the true character of every vegetable shines through. Taste the difference of sun-ripened integrity.

Community

Supporting local organic farms keeps our rural landscapes beautiful and our neighboring families thriving.
