About Our Clinic

Compassionate Care,
Exceptional Results

At BrightSmile Dental, we believe everyone deserves access to world-class dental care. Founded in 2009, our clinic has grown from a small practice into one of Toronto's most trusted dental centers, serving over 10,000 patients with dedication and expertise.

Our patient-first philosophy means we take the time to listen, understand your needs, and develop personalized treatment plans. We invest in the latest technology — from digital X-rays to laser dentistry — ensuring precise diagnoses and comfortable procedures.

Our Dental Services

From routine checkups to advanced cosmetic procedures, we provide a full range of dental services tailored to your needs.

General Dentistry

Starting at $80

Comprehensive exams, cleanings, fillings, and preventive care to maintain your oral health and catch issues early.

Teeth Whitening

Starting at $150

Professional-grade whitening treatments that brighten your smile by several shades in just one visit. Safe and effective.

Dental Implants

Starting at $500

Premium titanium implants that look, feel, and function like natural teeth. Restored confidence with lasting results.

Orthodontics

Starting at $300

Traditional braces and clear aligners to straighten teeth and correct bite issues for patients of all ages.

Root Canal

Starting at $400

Gentle, pain-free root canal therapy using advanced techniques. Save your natural teeth and eliminate discomfort.

Cosmetic Veneers

Starting at $600

Custom porcelain veneers crafted to transform your smile. Correct chips, gaps, and discoloration with natural-looking results.
