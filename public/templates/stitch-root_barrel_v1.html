Dig Deeper Into Our Roots

Root & Barrel began in a small community garden in 2012. Tired of the disconnect between supermarket shelves and the rich soil of our valley, we set out to build a bridge. What started as a three-person team has grown into a collective of over 40 independent growers dedicated to heritage seeds and sustainable cultivation.

People like Silas Thorne, who has been tending his family's apple orchard for three generations, and Maya Chen, whose regenerative greens have set new standards for local biodiversity. Every product in your basket has a face and a story behind it. We facilitate direct relationships so you know exactly who is growing your family's meals.

Sustainability isn't a buzzword for us; it's our operating manual. From compostable packaging and zero-emission delivery routes to supporting no-till farming methods that sequester carbon, we are committed to leaving the land healthier than we found it.
