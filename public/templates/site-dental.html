Our Services

Comprehensive Dental Care

From routine check-ups to advanced procedures, we offer a full range of dental services tailored to your needs.

General Checkup

$80

Comprehensive oral examination including X-rays, cleaning, and personalized treatment recommendations for optimal dental health.

Teeth Whitening

$200

Professional in-office whitening treatment that brightens your smile by up to 8 shades in a single session using safe, advanced technology.

Dental Implants

$1,500

Permanent tooth replacement solutions using titanium implants that look, feel, and function just like your natural teeth.

Orthodontics

$3,000

Braces and clear aligner treatments to straighten teeth and correct bite issues for a perfectly aligned, confident smile.

Root Canal

$600

Pain-free root canal therapy to save infected teeth using modern techniques that eliminate discomfort and preserve your natural tooth.

Pediatric Dentistry

$100

Gentle, child-friendly dental care in a fun, comfortable environment. We make every visit a positive experience for your little ones.
