A Fort McMurray Tradition

Montana's has been the beating heart of Fort McMurray's social scene for years. Nestled in the heart of downtown, we combine the warmth of a neighborhood pub with the excitement of a full-service sports bar. Whether you're catching the big game, enjoying a casual dinner, or celebrating a special occasion, Montana's is the place to be.

Our kitchen serves up hearty comfort food, from juicy burgers and crispy wings to fresh salads and signature poutines. Our bar is stocked with a wide selection of local and international beers, craft cocktails, and an impressive whiskey collection.
