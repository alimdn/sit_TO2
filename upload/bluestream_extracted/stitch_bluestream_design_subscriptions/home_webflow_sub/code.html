Common Inquiries

Everything you need to know about our partnership model.

How do subscriptions work? expand_more

Our subscriptions are flat-fee monthly models. This includes design updates, hosting, maintenance, and technical support, allowing you to budget with certainty.

Can I switch templates later? expand_more

Absolutely. Depending on your plan, you can request periodic design refreshes or full template migrations to keep your brand modern.

Is there a long-term commitment? expand_more

We offer both monthly and annual plans. Annual plans come with significant savings, but we maintain flexibility for teams that need it.

What happens to my data? expand_more

You own your content. While we provide the infrastructure and design, the data remains your intellectual property and can be exported at any time.
