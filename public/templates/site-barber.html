About Us

A Legacy of
Craftsmanship

Founded in 2009, Blade & Fade Barbershop has grown from a single chair in a small shop to one of the city's most trusted grooming destinations. Our philosophy is simple: every client deserves a personalized experience and a cut that reflects their unique personality.

Our team of eight expert barbers brings decades of combined experience, continuous training in the latest techniques, and an unwavering commitment to quality. We believe that a great haircut isn't just about the final look — it's about the entire experience from the moment you walk through our doors.
