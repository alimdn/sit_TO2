The Story Behind the Science

Founded by Layan Al Sharif, Lumina Wellness was born from a realization that the fitness industry was broken. Too many women were trapped in a cycle of "crash diets" and "warring with calories," leaving their metabolism shattered and their spirits defeated.

Layan transitioned from "Build with Protein" to focus on the holistic restoration of the female metabolism. By combining strength training with hormonal intelligence, she has helped hundreds of women find their way back to a body that works for them, not against them.
